Deep-Sea Blowfish

(Servings: 2 as a starter)

Ingredients

  • 1 deep-sea blowfish
  • 50 g sea bream or other white fish, absolutely fresh, filleted
  • 3-4 radishes
  • 2-3 spring onions
  • a few sprigs watercress
  • an eggcup full light soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed

Instructions

  1. THE MOST IMPORTANT thing here is not to use any of the blowfish whatsoever, since every single part of it is deadly in a very unpleasant way.
